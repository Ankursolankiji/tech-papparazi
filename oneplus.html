OnePlus Ace official launch scheduled for April 21

Peter Allen Clark | nov 15 /12:00pm ist

OnePlus is introducing a new smartphone in China, and it will be called Ace. A teaser appeared on the company’s Weibo profile showing three cameras on the back, flat sides and no alert slider.
The new OnePlus Ace will officially arrive on April 21, and while some reports claim it will be a rebranded Realme GT Neo3, it's more likely we are looking at the OnePlus 10R, expected to launch in India on April 28.
OnePlus Ace appears online, official launch scheduled for April 21 The alignment of the triple camera setup is in line with previous images we’ve seen, although the grooves are a cool addition to the design of the back. The images also revealed an IR sensor on top, and a USB-C port on the bottom, but they don’t show the front - we are yet to learn if the display will have a waterdrop notch or a punch hole.
